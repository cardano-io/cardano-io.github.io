Functionality

Staking

Information about stake delegation and stake pool registration.

The Cardano network is currently in the “Bootstrap Era”. Stake is automatically delegated to a pool of trusted nodes that maintain the network. During this time no block rewards is issued.

Once Cardano becomes fully decentralised as outlined in the roadmap for the Shelley release, the network will enter the “Reward Era”, and will operate in a completely decentralized and trustless mode. Ada token holders will then have the opportunity to earn a reward if they participate in the block creation process and are chosen as a slot-leader.

Stakeholders selected as slot leaders must be online in order to generate new blocks. Staking pools will therefore emerge during this era, allowing stakeholders with little stake to get rewards as stakeholders with large stake keep their nodes online to maintain the protocol in a decentralized fashion. Rewards earned by participating in a stake pool will be proportional to the size of the stake being delegated.

Stake Delegation

The Daedalus wallet will support staking and stake delegation when block rewards are introduced. A slot leader can transfer its right to generate a new block to a delegate.

The Daedalus wallet is being developed with features that will allow stakeholders to register new stake pools and delegate stake to other stakeholders or existing stake pools.

For more details about staking please see the Cardano documentation.

More information about stake delegation and stake pool registration will follow.
